Moveable shelving systems are a high density alternative to standard “static” shelf systems that proposes a simple, yet highly effective solution to space restrictions. This system involves laying down a track on the existing floor or optional decking surface, and mounting the shelf units, on carriages to allow the user to easily slide all of the units together via an electronic or manual control system. This eliminates the need for unused aisles and dramatically reduces the occupied floor space by doubling the storage density and efficiency. A moveable shelving system can be easily used for almost any storage application - such as:

-Archive Files
-Boxes/Bins
-Spare Parts
-Tools
-Weapons
-Evidence Storage
-Athletic Equipment
-Military Gear
-And Many More!
