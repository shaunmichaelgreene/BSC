Vertical Lifts & Carousels make use of available ceiling height to maximize storage density and reduce floor space required for storing materials. With intelligent software & inventory management systems custom-designed to integrate with your internal WMS/ERP systems, vertical lifts & carousels can dramatically improve storage efficiency, productivity, and accuracy.
Vertical Lift
Vertical Carousel
Utilizes custom sized shelf pans held in built-in storage slots spaced evenly on the front and rear interior walls of the lift. A central 4-chain supported extractor automatically stores and retrieves the desired pan or item at the touch of a button.
Standard sized shelf carriers rotate "ferris wheel" style to conveniently deliver shelves and materials to the access window for quick and ergonomic storage & retrieval.
